About Unilomio & My Mission

The Unilomio Vision

Unilomio is my world, a place where I build powerful, lightweight plugins to help other financially struggling founders thrive. The goal is to provide enterprise-level functionality without the enterprise price tag or bloat, making awesome web customizations and profitizing tools accessible to everyone.

The Purpose of My Github Showcase

As a solo founder, the plugins I create are the core intellectual property of my business. My plugins are super-economical, starting at just $29 (and I give some of them away for free). To protect the value and sustainability of Unilomio, the source code and my repositories remain private. The free plugins are dispatched as zip bundles to those who request for them. This helps me keep track of who's using the plugins. This showcase, however, serves as my public portfolio to demonstrate my skills and commitment, while protecting the core assets of my business.
