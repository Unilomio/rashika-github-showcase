About Unilomio & My Mission

The Unilomio Vision

Unilomio is my world, a place where I build powerful, lightweight plugins to help other financially struggling founders thrive. The goal is to provide enterprise-level functionality without the enterprise price tag or bloat, making awesome web customizations and profitizing tools accessible to everyone.

Why My Repositories Are Private

As a solo founder, the plugins I create are the core intellectual property of my business. To protect the value and sustainability of Unilomio, the source code remains private. This showcase, however, serves as my public portfolio to demonstrate my skills and commitment, while protecting the core assets of my business.
